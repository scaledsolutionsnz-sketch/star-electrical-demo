Star Electrical respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use the information you provide through this website.

1. What we collect

When you contact us through the website we collect the details you give us — typically your name, phone number, email address, suburb and a description of the work you need. We don't collect anything you don't choose to send us.

2. How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry, provide a quote, and carry out any work you book with us. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. Form submissions

Enquiry and feedback forms on this site are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service. Your details are transmitted to our email address and are not published on the website.

4. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies, primarily for basic functionality. We do not use them to track you across other sites.

5. Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you and request that we correct or delete it. Just get in touch.
